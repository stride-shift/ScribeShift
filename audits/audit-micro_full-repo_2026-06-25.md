**Full-repo dry run · 2026-06-25**

## Summary

This was a read-only micro audit of 38 modules across the full ScribeShift repository. 247 findings were classified across four disposition tiers: **89 human-global** (critical logic, security, data-integrity, or correctness issues requiring human judgment before touching), **56 flagged** (logical issues with lower blast radius or well-understood scope), **72 flow-auto** (safe mechanical fixes), and **2 lifecycle** items moved to the appendix. The most severe clusters are: RLS policy misconfiguration in Supabase migrations (authenticated users can write/delete any generation job or OAuth token row), TOCTOU credit races across all five credit-gated actions, silent empty-string returns from `geminiText` propagating into the database, and a non-functional daily-reminder cron (missing `postReminderEmail` export). No source files were modified; this report is the sole output.
