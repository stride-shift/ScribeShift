* Purpose
Present-state product context. Dormant/deferred features live in DEFERRED.org.

* What It Is
ScribeShift is an AI-assisted content platform: users define brands and content
types, generate social/blog/newsletter content with Gemini, preview it per
network, then schedule and publish to connected accounts (Facebook, Instagram,
LinkedIn, Twitter) and track post metrics. A credits system meters generation.

* Users & Roles
| Role | Scope | Can do |
|------|-------|--------|
| User | Own brands + content | Generate, schedule, publish, view analytics |
| Admin | Org-wide | Admin dashboard, invitations, credit management |
